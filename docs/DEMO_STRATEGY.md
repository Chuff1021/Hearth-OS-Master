# Demo Strategy

## Positioning

Hearth-OS Master is dealer network infrastructure for fireplace manufacturers.

The pitch is not just “better websites” or “cheaper software.” The pitch is:

> Give every authorized dealer a modern ecommerce superstore and operating system, preloaded with manufacturer catalogs, parts, service workflows, and payment rails.

## Manufacturer value

- stronger dealer digital presence
- more accurate product/model/parts representation online
- dealer network analytics
- easier dealer enablement
- higher parts and product throughput
- potential revenue share on payments/financing
- lower friction for dealers compared with ServiceTitan + QuickBooks stacks

## Dealer value

- branded ecommerce website
- service request intake
- product/order/quote request intake
- customer/job dashboard
- estimates/invoices/payment tracking
- less expensive than ServiceTitan
- more fireplace-specific than generic business software
